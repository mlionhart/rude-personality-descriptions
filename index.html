Unnecessarily Mean Descriptions of the Types

ENTJ

Extraverted iNtuitive Thinking Judging

ENTJs value efficiency above all other things. You may even feel rushed in their presence, for no apparent reason whatsoever - even if there is no work to be done.

ENTJs hate to be corrected; if you do so, prepare to be punched in the mouth.

Yes, they hate your messy car, house, and anything that resembles a state of apparent chaos. You'll possibly be punched for this as well.

Feel like crying? Don't. Seriously, it's not a good idea. You will be scolded for being so manipulative. To ENTJs, unless there's a death in the family, emotions are interpreted as a form of manipulation, and will either be ignored or out-right condemned.

Get in line, shut up, and get er done!

ENTP

Extraverted iNtuitive Thinking Perceiving

ENTPs are the type most likely to exhibit signs of hallucinogenic intoxication, even when they are stone sober. Their minds are so overactive and imaginative that they appear to be on drugs.

They are so unrealistic with Their intuitive perceptions that they often can't even tell if a speeding car would hurt them or not. "But, in my imagination the car sped right through me, as if I were a ghost!" they say.

ENTPs are mean, unless they have something they want from you - like attention, candy, fast food, money, and so on. The only way they know how to communicate is through manipulation. Welcome to the ENTP personality.

INTJ

Introverted iNtuitive Thinking Judging

INTJs take independence to a whole new level. In fact, I think the first person to ever legally marry their computer was an INTJ.

Indeed, they abhor the "common folk" and have no problem telling people they don't have time for them (unless you're interesting to them - good luck with that).

Feelings? No comprende, senor. Don't ever ask an INTJ how they "feel" about an issue; you will save yourself the embarassment, and the tense, long and awkward stare into your soul. A stare that dismantles every shred of self-esteem you have left as a human being; all because you felt it necessary to ask about an INTJs "feelings."

INTJs are a paradox, because they genuinely don't care what you think (about anything), but they DO care a great deal about their appearance - often taking hours to get groomed and ready just to go to the supermarket.

Like ENTJs, INTJs have no souls, but at least they keep mostly to themselves, so they don't usually do too much damage in the world.

INTP

Introverted iNtuitive Thinking Perceiving

INTPs are the best, most caring, most intelligent, personality type there is.

ENFJ

Extraverted iNtuitive Feeling Judging

ENFJs are natural leaders, if by leading you mean manipulating and guilt-tripping people into doing their bidding.

ENFJs are often very successful, due to an uncanny, almost supernatural ability to read souls, mind-screwing people into doing all of their hard work for them. This, and only this, is how they succeed.

The ENFJ is the most affectionate and loving personality type; the problem is they don't actually care about you, they simply enjoy the stimulation it gives them to communicate their feelings with hundreds of people per day. You're simply one out of the hundred.

If the ENFJ is left alone to fend for themself for more than a few minutes, they will have a panic attack and immediately call four to seven of their friends to vent their predicament with.

If you go more than a week without communicating with them, they will fear something terrible has happened to you, and will quickly call you to "check in."

If you don't agree with their view of the world, they will think you are a bad person, and will secretly write you off as a human being while still treating you with the exact same level of friendliness and affection as before.

ENFP

Extraverted iNtuitive Feeling Perceiving

Like ENTPs, ENFPs appear to be on drugs at all times, except they crave slightly more attention and praise.

If you are not looking at an ENFP, they will do something outrageous to get your eyes back on them.

They are very opinionated people, and they expect you to hang on to every word they say, because they just "know" they're right, even if they don't know anything about the subject they are talking about.

They are the type that sits in on one economics lecture, leaves after 15 minutes due to boredom, and then advises a Fortune 500 CEO on how to run their business based on what they "learned." ENFPs are the biggest dumbasses on the planet.

INFJ

Introverted iNtuitive Feeling Judging

INFJs are special unicorns. Just ask them, they will tell you.

Think you're a good person? The INFJ knows you are not. INFJs instinctively know that they are morally superior to everyone, and they will even advise the wisest sage on the "right" ways.

INFJs like to play the parent role - to everyone - their friends, siblings, and even their own parents. Again, they always "know" what's right.

Ever see a pissed off INFJ? You won't see them - that's the point.

Feel like giving an INFJ advice like you would a normal person? Don't even think about it. Would you also throw yourself into a bonfire just for the fun of it? I didn't think so. If you ever even think about giving an INFJ constructive advice (a phrase INFJs view as an oxymoron), you will regret it after the worst outburst you've ever seen from a grown adult.

But don't worry, you will awaken from a phone call at 2am later that night, from a guilty-sounding INFJ, who couldn't sleep until they apologized to you.

INFP

Introverted iNtuitive Feeling Perceiving

INFPs, like the ISFPs, are emo, but not quite as much. Their intuition seems to distract from their "bleeding heart" affliction to a degree - often providing rich, imaginative dreams involving fairies, elves, and magical places.

Feelings are all important to INFPs, even though they have no idea how they feel about anything.

But when the feelings come out un-filtered, like when provoked to anger, make sure you have adequate bullet-proof shielding to protect you. No, seriously.

INFPs take their responsibilities so seriously that they sometimes won't do them at all due to the stress.

INFPs can't say no. Seriously, even try to get them to mouth the word, "no" - they simply can't do it. It's simply a condition of being an INFP.

When you are in a bad mood or angry, the INFP will find a place to hide, hoping that their hiding from you will make you all better. INFPs are special snowflakes.

ESTJ

Extraverted Sensing Thinking Judging

ESTJs are the most common personality type, with ESFJs being a close second. ESTJs are bossy assholes, with a penchant for angry outbursts caused by nothing more than a harmless question. They can go to hell.

ESFJ

Extraverted Sensing Feeling Judging

ESFJs are the second most common personality type, right behind ESTJ's, but they are even more brainless and stupid - not to mention crazy. Not sure how to spot an ESFJ? Picture the most fake, vain, cliquey, ditzy, screwed up person you can possibly imagine. Got a picture in your head? Well, that's more or less an ESFJ. Sounds great, right? On to the next Type:

ISTJ

Introverted Sensing Thinking Judging

Try to think of the biggest stick-in-the-mud you know. How annoyingly rule-oriented they are, and bitingly critical of everything unfamiliar, or unlike them. In fact, oftentimes their "rule" is the more different you are from them, the more disgusting you are as a person.

But, not just disgusting, you're just not right - and you're probably also a traitor to the country.

Got a problem and need some advice or reassurance? Don't even think about coming to the ISTJ, unless it has to do with their expertise or their career field. Otherwise, you'll have a disturbed and annoyed ISTJ staring back at you, with a look of hatred on their face. Yes, they hate you in that very moment, but, it'll soon pass, as they get distracted by something else in their immediate environment.

To ISTJs, all that matters is appearances and material possessions. If you transform your lawn one summer, and it ends up looking better than their lawn, this will consume their thoughts until their lawn looks better than yours again, if they haven't already died of a heart attack from the ordeal.

Another thing - if you ever decide to be your own boss, and start your own business, to eventually become a successful entrepreneur, don't mention this to an ISTJ. They will either shrug off your idea as some sort of "child's play kool-aid sale," or tell you all of the ways in which you will fail before you even start.

Don't have your business budget planned out for the year 2053? Then you are an irresponsible piece of shit. Moreover, the ISTJ (as well as the ISFJ) sees all entrepreneurs as "unemployed." They don't respect people who are self-made and work for themselves. They only respect people who work for other people, because accomplishing anything by yourself instead of as part of a team is wrong, and you should probably be put into a concentration camp of some sort.

ISFJ

Introverted Sensing Feeling Judging

Have you ever met someone who is so disgustingly ass-kissing toward authority that it makes you want to puke? Well, this is the lucky day you get introduced to the ISFJ personality type!

This type will do everything is their power to please authority, and refuse to take any credit for anything they do, resulting in a feeling or bitterness toward life caused by being such push-overs. But, they will eventually blame life for all their failures.

They enjoy filling this empty void with material objects, hoping they can find happiness through material possession. This, of course, only leads to further emptiness.

Indeed, ISFJs are screwed in every sense of the word.

ESTP

Extraverted Sensing Thinking Perceiving

ESTPs relish fun and adventure. So much so that it negatively impacts every person in their lives.

If there was ever a type that fit the medical definition of a psychopath the most, it would be the ESTP.

They will stop at nothing to live their lives how THEY want; everyone else can go to hell. If you DARE to call out the ESTP on their selfish, destructive behavior, they will try to make you feel like a terrible person and scold you for "being such a dick."

Some ESTPs can be funny and entertaining, but that is literally all they are good for. Other than that, don't trust them within a 1000-mile radius.

ESFP

Extraverted Sensing Feeling Perceiving

ESFPs are always ready for a party, preferably while drunk or stoned. If you don't like to get drunk or stoned, followed by more drinking and "stoning," they will no longer like you, and assume that something is wrong with you.

To ESFPs, rational thinking is an oxymoron. Trying to get them to think rationally is like waking a dead rat with rat poison - it's not only pointless, it's incredibly stupid (what's wrong with you?). Anyway, fuck ESFPs.

ISTP

Introverted Sensing Thinking Perceiving

You know that 40-year-old father of three living down the street, with the minimum-wage job, no prospects, no future dreams or desires, and a skateboard as his main mode of transportation? Need I say more? Behold, the ISTP.

ISFP

Introverted Sensing Feeling Perceiving

Remember that chill emo kid in high school that lurched around, his long bangs dangling in his face while he walked? The same kid that made that depressing, black-and-white collage in tribute to the dead squirrel in the Senior parking lot? That kid is probably crying over dead roadkill as we speak. Meet the ISFP.
